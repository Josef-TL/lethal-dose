Everything that can be ingested has a "lethal dose", which is the amount to consume, where it would kill you. I base my calculations on the LD50 index. "The lethal dose for 50% of the population"

What exactly would be killing you in these scenarios? It differs from substance to substance. In most cases, it is a chemical in the food stuff, fx. caffeine in coffee and cola. However, in the case of bananas, you will die of radiation poisoning before potassium gets to you.
